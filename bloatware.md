## Live iso's that can be installed by size

### Requirements

* Has a live ISO
* Can be installed natively (not frugal)
* Straightforward installation

### Distros

#### Systemd

* Bodhi Linux (740 MB)
* Q4OS Plasma (931 MB)
* Trisquel Mini (950 MB)
